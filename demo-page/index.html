FAQ

Common questions.

How long does implementation take?
Most customers are live within 18 business days. Our implementation team handles data mapping, CRM configuration, and rep onboarding. You don't need an internal IT project — we've done this hundreds of times.
Does WorkHarder replace our CRM?
No. WorkHarder sits on top of your existing CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, or Microsoft Dynamics) and enriches it with AI insights and coaching. Reps stay in the tools they already use — we just make those tools smarter.
What integrations are supported?
WorkHarder has native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Outreach, Salesloft, Gong, Chorus, ZoomInfo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Slack, Microsoft Teams, and 40+ additional tools. Custom integrations are available on Enterprise plans.
Is our data secure?
WorkHarder is SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and CCPA compliant. Your data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise customers can opt for private cloud deployment with data residency controls.
What does the free trial include?
The 14-day free trial includes full access to the Growth plan features for up to 10 seats. You'll connect your CRM, import your live pipeline, and get AI deal scores and pipeline recommendations — no credit card required.
Can we start with one team and expand?
Absolutely. Many customers start with a single team or region as a pilot. Our pricing is per-seat, so you only pay for active users, and expansion takes hours — not another implementation project.
